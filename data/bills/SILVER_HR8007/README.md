# H.R. 8007 — "System Integrity through Licensed Vault Expansion and Resilience Act" (SILVER Act)
**Congress:** 119th, 2nd Session
**Introduced:** March 19, 2026
**Sponsors:** Rep. Russ Fulcher (R-ID), Rep. Chuck Harris (R-NC)
**Committee:** House Agriculture (CFTC oversight jurisdiction)
**Status:** Introduced — not yet passed
**Source:** https://www.congress.gov/119/bills/hr8007/BILLS-119hr8007ih.xml

## What It Amends
Section 5b(c)(2) of the Commodity Exchange Act (7 U.S.C. 7a-1(c)(2))
Affects: Derivatives Clearing Organizations (DCOs) — in practice, CME Group / COMEX

## Core Mandate
Forces every DCO to:
1. Develop "objective and transparent" criteria for approving precious metals depositories
2. Create a formal application process for new vaults
3. Approve a minimum of **2 depositories in each US time zone** (Eastern, Central, Mountain, Pacific)
4. Assess geographic diversity, competition, risk management, and storage costs in vault selection
5. Periodically assess physical settlement access for all US geographic locations

## Metals Covered
Gold, silver, platinum, palladium

## Current Vault Landscape (COMEX-approved)
All approved depositories are in the Eastern Time Zone (NY/NJ/DE corridor):
- Brinks Inc. (NY/NJ)

**Pacific Time approved vaults: 0**
